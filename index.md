# **According to [Mental Health America](https://mhanational.org/resources/quick-facts-and-statistics-about-mental-health/)**

***66%*** of these individuals Cannot afford to pay for Mental Health Service

## **In Oklahoma**

## **In Lawton / Fort Sill**
